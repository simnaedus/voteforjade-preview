Voting Information

Make your plan to vote.

Election Day is Monday, February 23, 2027.

1

Check your registration

Confirm your registration and polling place through the Cook County Clerk at cookcountyclerkil.gov/elections or Illinois Online Voter Registration at ova.elections.il.gov.

2

Vote early or by mail

Cook County offers early voting and vote by mail for suburban voters. Dates and locations for the February 2027 election will be posted here once the Clerk publishes them.

3

Vote on Election Day

Polls are open 6:00 a.m. – 7:00 p.m. on Monday, February 23, 2027. If you're in line by 7:00 p.m., you can vote.
